Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI logistics operations and services.

Our platform uses predictive analytics and real-time traffic data to dynamically adjust collection routes. This ensures our fleets take the most efficient paths, minimizing fuel consumption, reducing overhead, and cutting carbon emissions.

We primarily categorize waste into Recyclables (plastics, glass, paper), Organic/Compost, and General Waste. Our predictive models help balance the load of each truck based on the assigned zone's historical waste generation and current needs.

The analytics dashboard reflects near real-time data synced directly from our active fleet logs and computer vision checkpoints at sorting facilities. Overall metrics are refreshed roughly every 15 minutes.

Minor delays can occur due to unpredictable traffic conditions or localized route optimization. You can check the exact status on the Schedules page. If a pickup is missed entirely, please reach out to us via the Contact page.
